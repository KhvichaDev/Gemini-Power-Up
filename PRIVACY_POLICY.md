# Privacy Policy for Power-Up for Gemini

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## Overview
Power-Up for Gemini ("the Extension") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data.**

*   **Local Storage:** All data generated or used by the Extension (such as task lists, notes, chat history, settings, and code snippets) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Local Storage API.
*   **No External Servers:** The Extension does not transmit any user data to external servers, analytics platforms, or third parties.
*   **Permissions:** The requested permissions (such as `storage`, `alarms`, `notifications`, `clipboardWrite`) are used solely to provide the Extension's core functionality within your browser.

## Third-Party Services
The Extension operates on the Google Gemini website. Your interactions with Gemini are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. The Extension may interact with Google services (like Google Docs or Google Translate) solely at your request to perform specific actions (e.g., exporting a document).

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
